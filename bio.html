Biography - CV


Cesar Joaniquet (Barcelona 1988) is a talented saxofonist, composer and Big Band arranger. He performs all around Scandinavian countries and Spain in various settings and formations. As a versatile musician, he is known for his expressive performances, innovative compositions, and dynamic presence in the jazz scene. Cesar is a proud member of the prestigious Aarhus Jazz Orchestra, where he contributes with his unique sound, creative energy and modern compositions. With a passion for both traditional and contemporary jazz, Cesar has developed a distinctive sound and style of playing, improvising and composing.

Own works and projects:

  • 2025 'Live at Jazz Cava' - Quartet album featuring original compositions. Published by the Underpool Records (Barcelona). Recorded live at the iconic Jazz Cava in Vic, as part of the 26th edition of the Jazz Festival.
  • 2024 'DELTA' - Full concert piece, composed for Janne Mark, Arve Henriksen and Aarhus Jazz Orchestra, Vinterjazz 2024. Recorded the album "DELTA" at Brorson's Church Copenhaguen.
  • 2023 ‘Barken og Bladerne' - Composed for the ToneArt concert series 'The Secret Life of Trees'.
  • 2022 'Ama Sempre' - Big ensemble composition for Kor72 (300 singers) and Aarhus Jazz Orchestra. Premiered at the Christmas concert in Musikhuset Aarhus 2022.
  • 2022 'Boira' - Musical composition for ToneArt concert series ’Under nattens overflade’.
  • 2019 'Riot' - The work was premiered in Aarhus Musikhuset on the 3th September 2019 by the orchestra Blood Sweat Drum and Bass under the direction of Jens Christian 'Chappe' Jensen.
  • 2019 Zero Kestra - Thoroughly composed pieces of music for own orchestra, designated as 'European Jazz Highlight' during the Copenhagen Jazz Festival 2019.
  • 2018 'Sonum Cathedralis' - Large musical composition commissioned by and written especially for Aarhus Cathedral, St. Clemens Drengekor, Indra and Aarhus Jazz Orchestra under the direction of Carsten Seyer-Hansen.
  • 2018 'Sketches of Barcelona' - written for a rhythmic orchestra (Aarhus Jazz Orchestra) and international soloists. Jorge Rossy (ES), RJ Miller (USA) and Anna-Lena Schnabel (DE). The work premiered during the Winter Jazz Festival on February 21, 2018.
  • 2017 'Winds of Winter' - the work premiered at Aarhus Musikhuset on February 1, 2017 and then performed at Jamboree Barcelona on April 25, 2017.
  • 2016 Tsu-Na-Mi - Repertoire of own trio written for two winds and drums. Presented at Aarhus Jazz Festival 2016 and Tsuki Jazzfest.
  • 2015 Songbook - Trio project based in Aarhus, Denmark. Recorded the album "Songbook" with Brice Soniano on bass and Kasper Tom on drums.
  • 2014 Betania - Large ensemble work, written mainly for winds and string quartet.
  • 2014 In Motion - Compositions for saxophone, guitar and drums. Recorded the album "In Motion".
  • 2012 Smog Eyes (feat. Ted Brown) - Debut album recorded in New York. Features Michael Kanan on piano, Ted Brown on saxophone, Lim Yang on bass and Marton Juhasz on drums.
